About Me

Xipeng Qiu is a professor at the School of Computer Science, Fudan University. He received his B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Fudan University. His research interests include natural language processing and deep learning. He has published more than 60 top journal/conference papers (e.g., TACL, TKDE, T-ALS, ACL, EMNLP, IJCAI, AAAI, ICCV). He also leads the development of [GitHub] [Google Code] and FastNLP [GitHub].
